Norric Vattendom
Norric · Swedish water-judgment intelligence
Building · ingestion in flight
Canonical, queryable record of every Swedish water judgment — scored for disruption, with a 40-year clock on every villkor. Joins every vattendom to its operator orgnr (Bolagsverket) and a geocoded watercourse (Lantmäteriet). Each villkor is tracked against the 40-year omprövning clock introduced in the 2019 Miljöbalken reform — turning a regulatory tsunami in slow motion into a forecast operators, lenders, insurers, and kommunala VA-bolag can plan against. Disruption score per dam, per operator, per watercourse. Built on the same Bolagsverket spine as Kreditvakt and Sigvik.
⊘ Ingestion of Mark- och miljödomstolarna corpus in flight. No live API yet. Domain not registered.

Building
Thesis
Norric AB is a private holding company building a real-time statistical model of commercial Sweden. Each operating product — Kreditvakt for corporate insolvency, Sigvik for the BRF housing stock, SIGNAL for municipal procurement, Vattendom for Swedish water rights and hydropower regulatory exposure — ingests data directly from authoritative Swedish primary sources and contributes a calibrated signal to a shared intelligence core. The public surface of that core is Norric Pulse: a weekly nowcast published Mondays at 06:00 Europe/Stockholm, two days after the previous week closes.

The operating thesis is that in the AI era, intelligence is abundant but three things remain scarce: elapsed time spent ingesting, regulatory permission to ingest, and institutional trust in what is published. Norric optimises for all three.
